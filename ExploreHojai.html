What Is This Platform

Hyper-local travel, powered by the community

Verified Traveler

Share Your Story

The backbone of the platform. Any person — visiting tourist, local resident, or researcher — can become a Verified Traveler with a simple phone OTP. Full contribution access immediately.

  • Post experience stories with photos and ratings
  • Create Public, Private, and Collaborative tour lists
  • Ask questions and answer any community question
  • Rate and review places, food, accommodations, guides
  • Upvote the best answers to surface community wisdom
  • Earn badges — Food Critic, Hidden Gem Hunter, Local Voice
  • Convert private trip plans to public itineraries post-visit

Verification: Phone OTP + email confirmation. No ID required. Takes under 2 minutes.

Authorized Guide

Turn Knowledge Into Income

Professional and semi-professional local guides who are vetted and approved by Admin. They get everything a Verified Traveler has — plus a full professional presence and booking capability.

  • Full Verified Traveler capabilities
  • Dedicated guide profile page in the Guide Directory
  • Post official tour offerings with pricing and availability
  • Accept direct booking inquiries from travelers
  • Guide badge on all answered questions for trust signal
  • Guide Dashboard with booking and review analytics
  • Specialties: Agarwood, Heritage, Food Walks, Nature Treks

Verification: ID upload + experience proof. Admin reviews within 3–5 business days.

Admin

Own the Ecosystem

Full platform control. You curate the content, vet the guides, moderate the community, and steer the platform's growth. Initially a single-person role — expandable to a team.

  • Approve or reject guide applications with feedback
  • Moderate all content — remove, edit, or feature any post
  • Pin the best Q&A answers and feature top tour lists
  • Manage business listings and partnership accounts
  • Full analytics dashboard — traffic, users, content performance
  • Issue badges manually to recognize contributors
  • Manage platform categories, tags, and seasonal content

Admin access is private. A separate admin dashboard URL with secure login. Expandable to a moderation team.

Content Architecture

Six content pillars, one complete picture

Every piece of content falls into one of six curated categories — giving travelers a complete picture of Hojai without any gap.

💎

Hidden Gems

Beyond the top 3 tourist spots. Ancient temples in villages, local waterfalls, scenic drives, quiet river ghats — places only locals know.

🍛

Culinary Trail

Food changes every 50 km in India. Named vendors, specific dishes, traditional dhabas, breakfast culture, and seasonal street food specialties.

🏛️

Culture & Heritage

Festivals with exact dates, traditional arts, local music, the diversity of Assamese, Bengali, tribal, and Muslim communities.

🚌

Logistics & Practicality

How to reach Hojai from anywhere, local transport with rates, accommodation options, ATMs, network coverage, emergency contacts.

🌿

Nature & Outdoors

Rivers, forests, viewpoints, trekking routes. Seasonal accessibility, safety info, and wildlife sighting reports from the community.

🌳

Dedicated section for Hojai's world-famous Agarwood industry — factories to visit, grading explained, ethical sourcing, artisan interviews.

Hojai's Unique Asset

The Agarwood Capital of India

Hojai is home to one of India's most significant Agarwood (Oud) industries — a fragrant wood worth more than gold by weight, yet almost completely invisible online. Explore Hojai will be the first platform to document this extraordinary industry for the world.
